# Channel Targeting Feasibility Report

## Executive Brief

### Top Opportunities

1. LinkedIn Ads is the strongest paid-media fit. It maps the source into Location, Company industry, Company size, Job title, Job function (+1 more; see appendix); verify audience size and dynamic picklists before spend.
2. The biggest missing unlock is first-party/account targeting. Named accounts, customer/contact lists, website audiences, and suppression lists would materially improve precision.
3. Microsoft Advertising is a strong secondary path. Treat as demand-capture, contextual, or profile-derived tests unless authenticated checks prove tighter persona reach.
4. Search and custom-segment tests have usable demand language: workflow automation, revenue operations, marketing operations. Use these as intent/proxy signals, not proof of exact buyer reach.
5. Google Ads / YouTube, Reddit Ads, Google Display & Video 360, X Ads, TikTok Ads, Meta Ads: Facebook / Instagram are experimental or situational. Use for message learning, content/context tests, retargeting, or amplification rather than precise B2B persona targeting.

### Channel Readout

- **Conditional best fit:** LinkedIn Ads
- **Strong secondary:** Microsoft Advertising
- **Experimental or situational:** Google Ads / YouTube, Reddit Ads, Google Display & Video 360, X Ads, TikTok Ads, Meta Ads: Facebook / Instagram
- **Low fit:** none
- **Activation readiness:** Partially ready

### Best Campaign Concepts

- LinkedIn Ads persona campaign: target Software, Professional services, Revenue Operations Manager, Marketing Operations Director, Marketing, Sales, Operations, Manager (+1 more; see appendix) with source-backed messaging.
- Search/custom-segment demand test: build tightly controlled tests around workflow automation, revenue operations, marketing operations with negative keywords and landing-page alignment.

### Missing Inputs That Would Improve Targeting

| Missing input | Why it matters | Affected platforms | Decision blocked |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Named accounts / account list | Required for account-level precision and suppression logic | LinkedIn, Google, Microsoft, Meta, Reddit, X, TikTok, DV360 where eligible | ABM precision and account coverage |
| First-party audience source | Enables retargeting, matched audiences, and sequencing | Platforms with eligible first-party audience fields | Warm-audience and suppression plan |
| Lookalike / similar-audience seed | Required to evaluate modeled expansion | Platforms with verified modeled-audience support | Modeled-reach testing |
| Exclusions, suppression lists, and negative keywords | Prevents wasted spend and inappropriate reach | Search and platforms with suppression controls | Launch safety and traffic quality |
| Budget or test range | Determines channel mix and minimum viable test design | All evaluated platforms | Spend planning |
| Primary conversion event | Defines optimization and success criteria | All evaluated platforms | Measurement plan |

### Important Caveat

All platform conclusions are registry-backed unless authenticated account checks were explicitly run. Registry-backed only — not account-confirmed. Verify picklists, audience size, campaign type, locale, policy constraints, first-party eligibility, and suppression logic before any campaign build.
